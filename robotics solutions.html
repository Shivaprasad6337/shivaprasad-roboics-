// Student Club

SJCE Robotics Club

We are a student-driven robotics club at SJCE where engineers, coders, and makers come together to design and build intelligent robots. Open to all branches — if you love building, you belong here.

🎯

Our Mission

To foster hands-on learning in robotics and AI, empowering SJCE students to build real intelligent systems — not just study them.

🤝

Who Can Join

Any SJCE student — CSE, ECE, Mech, or any branch. All you need is curiosity, a willingness to learn, and passion for building robots.

🏆

What We Do

Weekly build sessions, hackathons, inter-college robotics competitions, workshops on ML/ROS, and collaborative robot projects.
